BIOS

ASRock A320M-HDV R4.0

You have BIOS version 3.30, from 8/6/2019.

This MAY be the wrong BIOS when used with a Summit Ridge processor (AMD Ryzen 3 1200). Contact ASRock Support for confirmation. See the CPU Support List.

BIOS 2.30 was the last BIOS that supported this processor. See here.

The newest BIOS is version 4.00, dated 7/29/2020... but again, it may not be appropriate for your processor. See here.

memtest

Ryzen processors are notorious for memory compatibility issues.

Go to https://www.memtest86.com/ and download/run their free memtest to test your memory. Get at least one complete pass of all the 4/4 tests to confirm good memory. This may take many hours to complete.

The answer to this question... Freeze may appear because of changes in BIOS. I had to reset settings of BIOS to default and PC actually stopped freezing. Commands I used:

sudo modprobe nvram 
sudo dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/nvram
